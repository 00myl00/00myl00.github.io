📊 研究成果 / Research

游戏衍生动画对游戏本体的拯救型跨媒介反哺研究——以《赛博朋克:边缘行者》的逆势拯救为例

摘要： 本研究清洗并分析了 54,787 条 Steam 玩家中英文评论数据，通过关键词匹配与卡方检验，探究衍生动画如何在不进行技术修复的前提下重塑核心情感认同，并总结了“拯救型跨媒介反哺”生效的四大核心机制。

Salvage-oriented Transmedia Feedback of Spin-off Anime on Games: A Case Study of Cyberpunk: Edgerunners

Abstract: This study analyzed 54,787 cleaned Chinese and English player reviews on Steam using chi-square tests. It evaluates how a high-quality spin-off can rebuild emotional connection and reverse product reputation without software patches, defining four essential conditions for successful transmedia feedback.
